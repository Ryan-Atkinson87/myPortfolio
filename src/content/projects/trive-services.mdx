Local tradespeople rely on word of mouth or generic booking platforms that aren’t built around mobile service work. Trive Services is a purpose-built platform for operators who work from a van rather than a fixed premises.

The launch covers two services under one operator, Trive Aqua (aquarium maintenance) and Trive Cycles (bike servicing), sharing a single backend and admin while presenting as distinct branded experiences via subdomain routing.

Beyond the business, it is a portfolio piece in greenfield architecture: multi-tenant theming, a real booking flow with slot locking and proximity pricing, and a multi-agent Claude Code workflow coordinated through Notion and GitHub.
