Most of my work starts as a problem I actually have — fish tanks that need watching, training files that are a pain to edit, a robot my son wanted to control. I build the thing properly, then keep going until it’s something other people can use.

That means I’m comfortable across the stack: React and TypeScript on the front, NestJS, Spring Boot and FastAPI on the back, Postgres underneath, and Raspberry Pi, sensors and 3D-printed enclosures when the problem reaches into the physical world.
